about us

BorderBuddy was founded a decade ago by Graham Robins, Jr. Importing and exporting was in Graham’s blood, as his parents built the highly successful A&A Customs Brokers business back in 1979.

Graham’s concept for BorderBuddy was to simplify the customs process. BorderBuddy specializes in importing and exporting products to North America. With a dedicated focus on consumers and small businesses, BorderBuddy keeps information focused and easy for everyone to understand. Here is a little bit of our story, including how we have evolved over the years.

our mission

Graham has worked hard over the last dozen years to ensure that there is not just a nuts and bolts operating system, but an emotional operating system at the core of everything we do. Because business is about people, and if employees and customers are not feeling like their needs are being met, we’re doing something wrong.

We live in times of intense tech innovation and disruption. The concept from the start with BorderBuddy was to build not just a business, but a community, with purpose beyond profit. Since we are in the business of importing and exporting, our goal is to provide solutions to move things forward for our customers. Graham’s involvement in YPO (Young Presidents’ Organization) has instilled in him a holistic approach to leadership, with personal development and continual innovation as the focus.

2008

BorderBuddy launches as a side business to A&A

Graham joins YPO

YPO (Young Presidents’ Organization) is a global leadership community of chief executives driven by a belief that the world needs better leaders. Graham was proud to join this network that inspires and challenges one another based on open sharing and trust among a diverse community. He took inspiration from the lifelong learning and idea exchange at the core of YPO and committed to continual growth and innovation for BorderBuddy from that day on.

Duty Calculator launched/h3>

We developed and launched the unique, innovative Customs Duty Calculator because you, our customer, shouldn’t have to do math. This revolutionary tool means you won’t have to — when it comes to calculating duties and taxes. To this day, there is nothing comparable out there that makes calculations in real-time like this for importers and exporters.

2017

BorderBuddy is relaunched with a fresh concept and its own dedicated building in Surrey, British Columbia.

With the progression of e-commerce, we saw a need that needed to be filled. Not only were companies in need of import and export expertise, but consumers were now shipping and receiving goods as well on a smaller scale. So in addition to our B2B function, we made it a point to accommodate B2C as well, to meet customer demand. BorderBuddy is truly there to provide solutions for any customer, no matter how large or how small.

2018

Implemented EOS (Entrepreneurial Operating System)

Another layer of innovation for BorderBuddy was studying EOS and implementing it as a tool for managers and employees to discuss creating more ownership and buy-in at the staff level. The idea is that employees take an active role in helping achieve the company’s vision, and it has been really effective.

2019

Implemented KPI Checklists

After reading KPI Checklists by Bernie Smith, we actually had him come in and talk to us about KPI (Key Performance Indicator) strategies. We implemented weekly and monthly scorecards with KPIs that we track to help us overhaul our 90-day goals, as well as our annual goals.

Launched a Podcast

Building on BorderBuddy’s corporate culture of innovation, Graham started The Graham Robins Podcast , dedicated to helping today’s business leaders grow and scale their businesses. The conversations with these really versatile and fascinating thought leaders inspire actionable, powerful ideas about finding success in business and in life.

Future of BorderBuddy

At BorderBuddy, we strive to continually move forward and adapt to changes in the industry and evolving regulations, while always putting our customers’ needs first. There is a massive shift toward digitization at the moment. In anticipation of this trend, we want to optimize our operation to eliminate paperwork and redundant data to continue to streamline the customer experience.
